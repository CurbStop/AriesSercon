    in Mozambique

    We are an independent group of accountants and tax professionals based in Mozambique. We have been operating in the market for over 30 years, long enough to witness and be part of the incredible growth of the country.

    Together we have helped small and large businesses, novices and even veterans in finding and setting up a space for them in Mozambique, and in doing so have developed an acute understanding of the country's changing business dynamics.

    Mozambique has seen consistent growth through a decade-long trend strongly driven by the extractive industries and large infrastructure projects. However, opportunities exist for small and medium size business ventures. A long coastline and an ideal geographic location also make it attractive for investment and trade.

    All-in-one approach

    We put our strengths into an integrated service which helps our clients get started quickly.

    Our all-in-one approach removes operational obstacles by keeping all functional areas under one roof.

    Think about launching your business, applying for visas, wading through tax issues and managing your workforce, all through a single and cohesive interface.

    It is one of the main reasons why we have provided our expertise to over two hundred clients and continue to do so.
